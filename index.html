SpiritRelay V1.0.0


SpiritRelay has several features aimed at communication with spirits and for paranormal research:


  • Spirit writer (automatic writing/drawing) - 2 modes; freehand and auto
  • Ghostbox with additional White nose, sweep rate and tone/pitch settings
  • Written response (Word list with 1000 words)
  • Possibilty to save screenshots of any interesting communication
