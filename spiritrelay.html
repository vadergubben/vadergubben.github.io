SpiritRelay


SpiritRelay - a completely free Windows ITC-application - is used to communicate with spirits and for paranormal research. It's based on the principle of Automatic writing/drawing, and it features many functions to enhance your séances and other spirit communications:


  • Spirit writer (automatic writing/drawing) - 2 modes; freehand and auto

  • Ghostbox with additional White nose, sweep rate and tone/pitch settings

  • Written response (Word list with 1000 words)

  • Possibilty to save screenshots of any interesting communication
